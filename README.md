## 🚀 Featured Projects

> A curated selection — each solves a real problem I personally ran into.

---

### 🔵 [ATSPrecise](https://github.com/BharathLakkoju/atsprecise) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Live ↗](https://atsprecise.vercel.app)

> AI-powered ATS resume evaluator — upload your resume, paste a JD, get a precise compatibility score.

**Stack:** `Next.js` `TypeScript` `Drizzle` `Supabase` `Zustand` `AI`

Weighted scoring across keyword match, skills alignment, section completeness, and formatting quality. Includes gap analysis, filler-phrase detection, and role-specific improvement guidance.

---

### 🟣 [Job Hunter Web](https://github.com/BharathLakkoju/job-hunter-web) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Live ↗](https://job-hunter-web-three.vercel.app)

> Personal job intelligence dashboard — aggregates listings every 4 hours, scores them by hiring probability, sends Telegram alerts.

**Stack:** `Next.js` `TypeScript` `Node.js` `PostgreSQL` `Supabase` `Drizzle` `Zustand`

Scrapes Indeed, Naukri, and Wellfound. Deduplicates using URL hashing. Scores 0–100% on skill match, recency, location, and company type. Tracks full application lifecycle.

---

### 🟠 [SwiftMedia](https://github.com/BharathLakkoju/swiftmedia) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Live ↗](https://swiftmedia-nine.vercel.app)

> Browser-based media toolkit — compress images, videos, and PDFs, merge PDFs, convert images to PDF. No server uploads.

**Stack:** `Next.js` `TypeScript` `WebAssembly` `FFmpeg` `Framer Motion`

100% client-side processing via FFmpeg WASM. Animated, accessible UI. Handles JPEG, PNG, WebP, MP4, and PDFs.

---

### 🟡 [Job Scraper Pipeline](https://github.com/BharathLakkoju/job-scraper)

> Autonomous job application pipeline — scrape → AI-tailor resume per JD → track in Google Sheets → classify recruiter replies via Gmail.

**Stack:** `Python` `Flask` `Docker` `JobSpy` `OpenRouter (Llama 3.3 70B)` `Google Drive API` `Gmail API`

Multi-source scraping (LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, Glassdoor). AI resume rewriting per job. Hourly Gmail classification for interview/rejected/offer status.

---

### 🟢 [Dvelve](https://github.com/BharathLakkoju/Dvelve)

> Local-first AI research assistant — enter a topic, get a structured multi-source report from a 5-agent pipeline running on your machine.

**Stack:** `React` `Vite` `FastAPI` `Python` `SQLite` `Ollama` `Go`

Five specialized AI agents handle planning, search, synthesis, critique, and formatting. No cloud required.

---

### 🔴 [Flatmate](https://github.com/BharathLakkoju/flatmate) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Live ↗](https://adjustyouflat.vercel.app)

> Calendar-first shared living app — expenses, meals, tasks, groceries, and events for flatmates.

**Stack:** `Next.js` `TypeScript` `Supabase` `Drizzle` `Zustand` `Vitest` `Playwright` `Gemini AI`

OCR-powered receipt scanning via Tesseract.js. Google Gemini AI for smart household suggestions. Full test coverage.
